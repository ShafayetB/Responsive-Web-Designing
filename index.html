Food Items

Pizza

Pizza is a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese.

Burger

It is one or more cooked patties of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun.

Sandwich

It consists of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between slices of bread.

French fries

French-fried potatoes are batonnet or allumette-cut deep-fried potatoes.
